# HRIMS AI - Feature Roadmap (Phase 2 & 3)

> [!NOTE]
> This roadmap focuses on post-recruitment modules to deepen the platform's capabilities in employee lifecycle management, retention, and operational efficiency.

## 1. Payroll & Compensation Mastery
**Goal**: Move beyond basic salary structures to intelligent financial wellness and complex payroll scenarios.

- **Global Payroll Compliance Engine**:
  - Automated tax calculation updates for US/UK/India/Singapore.
  - Compliance alerts for minimum wage changes.
- **Deep Equity Planner**:
  - Vesting schedule visualizer for employees.
  - "Paper money" vs "Real money" scenario modeling for pre-IPO startups.
- **Expense Intelligence**:
  - AI-based flagging of anomalous expense claims.
  - Automated receipt parsing and categorization.

## 2. Advanced Performance & Growth
**Goal**: Shift from "managing performance" to "accelerating growth".

- **Real-time Feedback Loop ("QuickFives")**:
  - Slack/Teams integration for instant micro-feedback throughout the week.
  - AI synthesis of micro-feedback for quarterly reviews.
- **Skill Gap AI**:
  - Automatically identifies skill gaps based on role requirements vs. employee profile.
  - Recommends specific internal mentors or courses.
- **Project-Based Performance**:
  - Performance reviews linked specifically to project milestones rather than just dates.

## 3. Operations & Facilities (The "Physical" Workplace)
**Goal**: Streamline the physical and digital workspace experience.

- **Intelligent Desk Booking**:
  - Hot-desking map with team-clustering (book seats near your team).
  - Usage heatmaps in the Admin dashboard.
- **Asset Lifecycle Tracker**:
  - Automated refresh reminders for laptops (e.g., "MacBook Pro M1 due for upgrade in 30 days").
  - Hardware health monitoring integration.
- **Visitor Management System**:
  - QR code passes for office guests.
  - Automated NDA signing flow on iPad kiosk mode.

## 4. Workforce Analytics & Prediction
**Goal**: Move from "what happened" to "what will happen".

- **Flight Risk 2.0 (Predictive)**:
  - Uses non-invasive signals (e.g., drop in calendar participation, late timesheets) to predict burnout.
  - *Privacy-first implementation required.*
- **Organizational Network Analysis (ONA)**:
  - Visualizes communication flow to identify "isolated" teams or "bottleneck" key individuals.
- **Diversity & Inclusion Index**:
  - Real-time tracking of pay equity, promotion velocity across demographics.

## 5. Learning & Development (L&D)
**Goal**: Make learning addictive and relevant.

- **Micro-Learning Feeds**:
  - TikTok-style scroll of internal knowledge bites/videos.
- **Certification Vault**:
  - Blockchain-verified certificates for internal achievements.
- **Mentorship Matchmaker**:
  - Tinder-style matching for mentors/mentees based on skills and career goals.

## 6. Employee Engagement & Culture
**Goal**: Build a "tribe", not just a workforce.

- **Culture Coins (Gamification)**:
  - Earn points for helping peers, writing docs, interviewing.
  - Redeem for company swag or charity donations.
- **Virtual Watercooler**:
  - Prompt-based social channels (e.g., "What's the best thing you ate this weekend?") with AI conversation starters.
